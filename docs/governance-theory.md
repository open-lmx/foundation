# Governance Theory

Governance is the system of principles, policies, controls, rules, and evidence used to guide and constrain action.

Governance must be executable, observable, and auditable.

## Governance Hierarchy

Law
Safety
Mission
Principles
Policies
Standards
Controls
Objectives
Tasks
Actions

## Conflict Rule

A lower layer cannot override a higher layer.

## Governance Questions

Every governed action should answer:

- Who acted?
- What was acted on?
- Why was it allowed?
- Which policy applied?
- What evidence was used?
- What changed?
- Who approved it?
- Can it be reversed?

# Identity Theory

Identity is persistent.

Names may change.
Owners may change.
Locations may change.
Providers may change.
Deployments may change.
Identity must remain stable.

## Identity Requirements

Every asset must have:

- Stable identifier
- Issuer
- Subject
- Type
- Creation timestamp
- Verification method
- Lifecycle status

## Recommended Forms

- DID
- Verifiable Credentials
- URI identifiers
- UUID identifiers
- Organization-scoped identifiers

## Identity Rule

No action may be trusted unless the actor, subject, tool, and authority are identifiable.

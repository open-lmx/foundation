# Open-LMX Capability Data Models

Capability is the bridge between learning and work.

Open-LMX treats capability as the evidence-backed ability of a person, team, agent, institution, or organization to perform work in a defined context.

## Purpose

Capabilities connect competencies, credentials, evidence, and experience to roles, jobs, projects, and career progression.

## Core Capability Types

- Capability
- CapabilityLevel
- CapabilityRequirement
- CapabilityEvidence
- CapabilityProfile
- CapabilityGap
- CapabilityAssessment
- CapabilityRecommendation

## Capability Graph

```text
Assessment Evidence
    ↓
Competency
    ↓
Capability
    ↓
Capability Requirement
    ↓
Role
    ↓
Job
    ↓
Career Path
```

## Rule

Employers do not hire courses. They hire capability.

Open-LMX must make capability visible, comparable, evidence-backed, and portable.

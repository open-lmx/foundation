# Open-LMX Glossary

## Asset

A governed entity that can be identified, versioned, evaluated, related, trusted, used, or retired.

## Identity

A persistent identifier associated with an asset.

## Lifecycle

The states through which an asset progresses over time.

## Relationship

A typed connection between assets.

## Policy

A rule or constraint that governs behavior.

## Governance

The framework of principles, policies, standards, controls, decisions, and evidence used to guide action.

## Trust

Confidence derived from evidence, performance, provenance, verification, and review.

## Evaluation

The process of assessing capability, quality, reliability, compliance, risk, or fitness.

## Certification

A formal declaration that an asset conforms to defined requirements.

## Interoperability

The ability of assets, systems, organizations, and protocols to operate together through shared standards.

## Agent

An autonomous or semi-autonomous system capable of perceiving, reasoning, deciding, and acting.

## Tool

A capability invoked by an agent, human, workflow, or application.

## Skill

A reusable capability pattern that can be evaluated and composed.

## Model

A computational model that provides predictive, generative, analytical, or reasoning capabilities.

## Registry

A governed catalog of assets and associated metadata.
